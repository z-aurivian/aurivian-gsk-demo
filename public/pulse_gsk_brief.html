Rheumatologists are initiating Benlysta after 2+ lines of failure for lupus nephritis. The label supports earlier use — and the biology makes a stronger argument at line 1.

The earlier-line biological rationale is scientifically stronger: BLyS suppression at diagnosis interrupts B-cell pathology before irreversible renal fibrosis develops.
